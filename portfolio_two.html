Science Fiction Room

We were required to create a series of hallways leading into a control room, along with coding a script for making the player seamlessly transition from the end of the hallway back to the start. The theme we were given was science fiction. After finding some reference photos that I liked, I began designing it in Maya amd afterwards, I textured the hallway design using Adobe Substance Painter.

I then created the two other different rooms, which are the control room and also a storage room.

I then created a reactor to place inside the control room
