Mix & Match

Mix & Match is a click game made solely by me using Unity Engine. The ganme starts with a set of random numbers with icons hiding them being generated, and the player has to click to turn over the icon and match the numbers in order to win the game. I also created my own icons using Adobe Illustrator.

I also created a firebase to store the player data, as well as the leaderboard using firebase authentication and the realtime database.The player dataset consists of a randomly generated player id, name, password, email and playerStatus, while the leaderboard consists of the playerStatus, score and stage.

Game Play

Players will first log in or create their own accounts, afterwards they will be brought to the game start menu where they can view their own profile, check the leaderboard or adjust the music. They may also log out if they wish. The game starts immediately after clicking the start button and players have 1 minute to complete as many matches as possible. In each stage, there is a total of four matches to be made. Players can choose to continue onto the next stage, restart the entire game or quit half-way.
