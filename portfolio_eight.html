Card Game

These are a series of graphics I created for my own monopoly-style card game. I created a poster to advertise this game, along with a game board and four different cards to showcase. I went with a Hollywood theme, with the main color scheme being a bold red and the cards coming from classic movies.

I then created a game board, both front and back, inspired by the game of Monopoly

I then created a series of four cards, inspired by Dorothy, Snow White, Alice In Wonderland and Charlie and The Chocolate Factory.
