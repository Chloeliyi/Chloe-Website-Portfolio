Mix&Match

We were required to make our own island, with a water shader and waterfall, a giant tree and flags. I was inspired by Tibetan architecture, and created my own temple based on the design, along with a small harbor. I then created my own flag design and made a giant mystic tree as a vocal point with flag arounds it and some statures. I then populated the island with trees and a fog.

I created the temple and harbor using Maya and then textured them in Adobe Substance Painter

For the flag, I wanted it to be series of flags tied to poles that could be placed around the tree. So I created the pole and flags in Maya and then brought it over to Unity where I textured it.
