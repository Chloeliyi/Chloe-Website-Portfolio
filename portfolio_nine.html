Row Of Shophouses

The assignment for this was to create a 3D background of any theme to be placed in Adobe Aero. I wanted to recreate an iconic setting of Singapore, so I referenced the iconic rows of shophouses and redesigned them in Adobe Illustrator, along with a road, clouds, trashbins and signs, and finally some cars.

This is the main background I designed, using my own color scheme. It had to be divided into several different jpgs to bring into Adobe Aero
