Fantasy Room

We were required to create a series of hallways leading into a room with a table and chairs, along with a giant crystal as centerpiece. The main theme of this design that I went with was medieval fantasy.

After finding some reference photos that I liked, I began designing it in Maya.

I then textured the design using Adobe Substance Painter.

Photos:

I first started by white blocking all the different pieces of the hallways using Maya as my guide, then I designed the hallway because I wanted to reuse it for the different hallway pieces. I had three different hallway pieces, a straight hallway, a hallway with a turn and then a hallway with a downward slope.

After that I designed out the main room with a table.

After that, I made the crystal, both a low poly and a high poly version.

I then textured both my hallways, along with the crystal.
