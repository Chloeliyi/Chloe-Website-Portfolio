Road Master Mechanic

"Road Master Mechanic" is an action and casual PC game that targets car enthusiasts and young adult gamers. Players take on the role of a skilled mechanic working at Master Garage. The goal is to become a master mechanic and a car racer. To achieve this, players must earn money by repairing cars by using junk in the Junkyard, purchasing, and upgrading their own cars, and participating in exhilarating races at various racetracks.

Plot

The player has been hired by Master Garage; a renowned car repair shop known for fixing cars. The Boss of Master Garage assigns the player as a car mechanic, but the player harbours dreams of becoming a true master mechanic and a talented car racer. To fulfil their ambitions, the player embarks on a thrilling journey, starting with repairing cars in need of servicing. By earning money from these repairs, the player can gradually buy better cars from the Dealership and eventually participate in high-stakes races at different Racetracks.

Target Audience

Car enthusiasts and young adult gamers who enjoy action and casual gameplay experiences.

Player Metrics

• Total Money Earned: Measures the player's progress and success in the game. • Cars Repaired: Tracks the number of cars successfully repaired by the player. • Races Won: Reflects the player's racing skills and achievements on the track.

Time

Game : 20 minutes
Each level: Around 5-7 minutes
